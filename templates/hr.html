HR Policies

Casual & Sick Leave

Employees are entitled to 12 leave days per year, covering both Sick Leave and Casual Leave. Any unused leave days will be converted to Earned Leave.

  • Reimbursement Option: Unutilized leave days at the end of the year will be reimbursed at the employee’s standard salary rate.
  • Carry-Forward Option: Employees may carry forward unused leave to the following year, subject to a maximum of 90 days.

Bereavement Leave

Maximum of 1 day per year, subject to manager’s discretion. Timesheet update is required.

Maternity & Paternity Leave

  • Maternity Leave: As per local government laws.
  • Paternity Leave: 5 days of paternity leave.
