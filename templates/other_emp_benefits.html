Other Employee Benefits

Standard Working Hours / Shift Coverage

Shift Time (IST) Transporation Allowance
Shift 1 1:30 PM – 10:00 PM Transportation from Office to Home (Female Employees Only)
Shift 1 After Hours Post 12:00 Midnight Transportation from Office to Home (All Employees)
Overtime Pay

Overtime pay will be provided only with prior approval from management and TechProjects client. Hours beyond 8/day or 40/week may be considered overtime.

Gratuity

Payable to employees completing at least 5 years of service with the company.

Wellness Program

Employees working from GCC center will have access to the in-campus gym once operational.

Provident Fund & Statutory Welfare

The company contributes up to ₹1,800 per month to PF. Employees must match this contribution and may opt to contribute up to 12% of basic salary.

Training Program

Freshers and probationary employees undergo skill development programs. On-the-job training is conducted by subject matter experts at GCC.
